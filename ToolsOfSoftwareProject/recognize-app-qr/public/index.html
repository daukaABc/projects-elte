Our program will give institutions like universities and businesses the ability to track their students or employees with a virtual profile on website. It will be able to relay information such as: name, gender, time entered, etc. to a database after being scanned QR-Code by a camera. This will allow the institutions to be able to perform functions like attendance scanner sheet, tardiness.
